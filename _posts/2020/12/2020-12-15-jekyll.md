## toyjhlee.github.com 의 로컬 환경 설정

1. `gem install bundler jekyll`
2. `git clone https://github.com/toyjhlee/toyjhlee.github.com`
3. `cd toyjhlee.github && bundle install`
4. `bundle exec jekyll serve`
